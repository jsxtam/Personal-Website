Cantata Pansophical

Doty, Take This Down

An Exandrian Musical II
(working title)

Recording viola for this fanbased musical project based on the musical Dear Evan Hansen and the webseries Critical Role. Catata Pansophical (CPan) are a group of dedicated musicans, artists, and audio engineers brought together being fans (critters) of Critical Role. In 2018, CPan released their first album: Vox Machina, An Exandrian Musical; an effor of over 100 singers and artists to tell part of Critical Role's first campaign story through the music of the Hamilton musical.

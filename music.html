Cantata Pansophical

Doty, Take This Down

Recorded viola for this fan musical project based on the musical Dear Evan Hansen and the webseries Critical Role. Catata Pansophical (CPan) is a group of dedicated musicans, artists, and audio engineers brought together being fans (critters) of Critical Role. In 2018, CPan released their first album: Vox Machina, An Exandrian Musical; an effort of over 100 singers and artists to tell part of Critical Role's first campaign story through the music of the Hamilton musical.
